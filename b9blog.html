A Special Thanks

Welcome and about

When I first saw the great 1960's television series, Lost in Space, I was about the same age as one of the characters, Will Robinson. My father had gone upstairs to get the VHS tapes and came downstairs with a recording of the episode, "Revolt of the Androids". I was immediately hooked, and watched the episodes as often as possible, and just as often build tiny effigies of the beautiful robot out of cardboard boxes, and play with them untill my mother got tired of the mess and threw them away. I always knew that I would build a full-scale replica one day, and the time has come (my mom won't throw this one away).
