Thanks to the Builders

A Special Thanks

Ears

The ears I will probably end up molding myself... either with clay, sheet metal, plastic, or ceramic.

Ear Posts

If I mold the ears myself, I will probably mold the posts the same way. I just have to make sure they are hollow enough to fit the motor mechanisms.

Ear Motors

For the ear motors, I think I will run structural wire through the ear posts that connect to the edge of a concealed circular base of the ear, as a motor in the radar twists, the wire will twist the ear as well. It is an interesting way around using gearboxes.

Radar Base

Just as many parts on the stunt version of Robot B9, my radar will most likely be made out of cardboard. I will smooth a hardening clay or liquid paper mache over it, sand, prepare, and paint.
