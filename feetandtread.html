Thanks to the Builders

A Special Thanks

Feet

Both mirror identicals of each other except for the soil sampler hatch. Look closely and you will see a rectangular hatch. This is where the soil sampler comes out. I will most likely create the feet using sheet metal, but possibly out of wood. The side panels are identical to each other. I will make the soil sampler out of PVC and it will run on a simple motor for retracting and spinning.

Knees

The knees start at the point in the legs where the two metal plates extrude. The knee hinges are flat, and attatched to the side, if you look close you can see it. The plates are thick, and I do say that I do NOT want to cut that thick of metal, so I will stack cardboard and cover it with a paper mache or clay, smooth, prepare, and paint.
