WHO ARE WE?

Rexburg Hero Party is an organization run by students at BYUI, this being the case our prices are lower than other organizations ($25 per hour), and services still quality rich! We provide services to charity events such as schools and other programs, and we provide fun and family friendly entertainment to everyone.

Austin Bowler

Hey there, Austin Bowler here! I've been working as Spiderman in Las Vegas, Nevada doing both charity and paid events since March of 2016. After thoroughly learning the trade, I am excited to come to Rexburg, Idaho and share the fun that I've had with you! In my free time, I usually repair vintage and antique typewriters, spend time with my family, and work on building a replica of the robot from Lost in Space. I learned the values of hard work and time management while working three different jobs simultaneously with entrepreneurs: math tutoring at Easy as Pi Mathematics, hard labor in the Las Vegas heat with Will's Landscaping, and of course, a Spiderman actor at Party Ever After LV (www.partyeverafterlv.com), and (https://www.facebook.com/PartyeverafterLV).
