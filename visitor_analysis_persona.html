The RPC Studio

URL: therpc.studio

Visitor Analysis

It looked very nice, but a few of the buttons and text ran into each other. The html script was obviously formatted for mobile, but text and buttons were not formatted very well. They most likely used a desktop to mobile converter.

Persona

I was shocked when I looked at The RPC Studio's web site and found no hint of any persona! Literally, there is no "about" tab, or any information abouth where they are even based. For an online store it seems pretty scary... I, however, have made business with them, throwing caution into the wind and doing something that no normal person would do. This is why persona is important; if you do not have it, nobody buys form you. Unless you are crazy like me.
