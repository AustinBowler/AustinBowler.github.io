Power Pack

The power pack was made out of some old military equipment that some dudes in Hollywood somehow got their hands on. Therefore, it is hard to reproduce. But thank goodness for the B9 Robot Builders Club! Below are three links to the club's Vendor page, and on the Bill K. and Rod R. page there is more information about the power pack (and probably more accurate). If I dont buy one of them, I guess I'll build a new one with eyes burning and hands shaking.
